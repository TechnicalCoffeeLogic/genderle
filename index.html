What is my standard?
You are probably wondering, “Who the heck gave you the authority to decide who is a man and who is a woman?” Well, no one gave me the authority; I am simply proclaiming the truth.

The only one who has such authority would be the creator of all things, which is none other than Jesus, the Son of God. “He is the image of the invisible God, the firstborn of all creation. For by him all things were created, in heaven and on earth, visible and invisible, whether thrones or dominions or rulers or authorities—all things were created through him and for him. And he is before all things, and in him all things hold together.” (Colossians 1:15-17)

Jesus, the Son of God, “emptied himself, by taking the form of a servant, being born in the likeness of men. And being found in human form, he humbled himself by becoming obedient to the point of death, even death on a cross, [and has risen from the dead]. Therefore God has highly exalted him and bestowed on him the name that is above every name, so that at the name of Jesus every knee should bow, in heaven and on earth and under the earth, and every tongue confess that Jesus Christ is Lord, to the glory of God the Father.” (Philippians 2:7-11 )

He has been given all authority in heaven and on earth (Matthew 28:18), He is the only one with the authority to define man, woman, and gender.

You can attempt to do your own thing, but you are His creation. You can say “there is no God”, but you are wrong. My suggestion is to submit to the Son of God, who is the true King, and follow Him.
